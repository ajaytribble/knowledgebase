## Key Features

### Smart Knowledge Management
- **AI-Powered Search**: Instantly find relevant information using natural language
- **Context-Aware Responses**: Get accurate answers based on your approved content
- **Knowledge Capture**: Automatically save and organize responses for future use

### Efficient RFx Response
- **Portal Integration**: Answer questionnaires directly in vendor portals
- **Project Management**: Track progress and maintain version control
- **Team Collaboration**: Work together with role-based access and permissions

### Team Workflows
- **Shared Workspace**: Central hub for team collaboration
- **Real-time Updates**: Stay synchronized across all platforms
- **Version Control**: Track changes and maintain response history
