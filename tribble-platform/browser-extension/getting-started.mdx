## Quick Setup Guide

1. **Install the Extension**  
   * Download from the Chrome Web Store  
   * Click "Add to Chrome" and confirm the installation  
   * Compatible with most Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Edge, etc.)

2. **Access and Sign In**  
   * Pin the extension: Right-click the Tribble icon in the extension menu and select "Pin"  
   * Open the extension: Click the Tribble logo in your toolbar  
   * Sign in with your Tribble account credentials

<Note>
The Tribble browser extension works with Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers like Brave and Edge.
</Note>

## Using the Extension

### Accessing Tribble

1. Click the Tribble icon in your browser toolbar
2. Sign in with your Tribble credentials if not already logged in
3. The extension panel will open, showing your recent projects and options

### Key Features

1. **Project Creation**
   * Create new projects directly from web pages
   * Add questions and notes to your projects
   * Organize content by topic or category

2. **Quick Search**
   * Search across all your organization's knowledge
   * Get instant answers while browsing
   * Access relevant documents and resources

3. **Content Capture**
   * Save important web pages and snippets
   * Add notes and comments to saved content
   * Share insights with your team

## Best Practices

1. **Pin the Extension**
   * Keep Tribble easily accessible in your toolbar
   * Right-click the icon and select "Pin"

2. **Regular Sign-in**
   * Stay signed in for seamless access
   * Check your login status if features aren't working

3. **Project Organization**
   * Create clear project names
   * Use consistent naming conventions
   * Add descriptive tags when possible

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

1. **Extension Not Working**
   * Ensure you're signed in
   * Try refreshing the page
   * Check your internet connection

2. **Sign-in Problems**
   * Clear your browser cache
   * Ensure you're using the correct credentials
   * Contact support if issues persist
