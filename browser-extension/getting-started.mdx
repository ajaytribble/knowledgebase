## Installation

Learn how to install the Tribble browser extension in your preferred browser.

### Chrome Installation

1. Visit the Chrome Web Store
2. Search for "Tribble" or use our direct link
3. Click "Add to Chrome"
4. Follow the prompts to complete installation

### Firefox Installation

1. Visit Firefox Add-ons
2. Search for "Tribble" or use our direct link
3. Click "Add to Firefox"
4. Follow the prompts to complete installation

## Initial Setup

After installation, you'll need to:

1. Click the Tribble icon in your browser toolbar
2. Log in with your Tribble account
3. Configure your preferences
